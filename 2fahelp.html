Help for Two-Factor Authenticated Accounts

Goto your Microsoft Account Settings
Scroll-down and click on "Security" then Click "Additional security options"
Scroll-down and click on "Create a new app Password"
Copy the Application Password and use that to login as if it was your normal password

YOU MAY DELETE THE PASSWORD, BUT YOU WILL HAVE TO CREATE
A NEW ONE TO LOGIN.
THE DEVELOPER DO NOT HAVE ACCESS TO YOUR ACCOUNT USING THIS PASSWORD!
